# Vivarium

A 3D simulation sandbox — a contained living world.

- Discover, tame, and collect creatures with unique abilities.
- Breed creatures to pass down traits and see emergent mutations.
- Gene splicing as endgame depth layer.

Toy-first: build the sandbox and creatures, figure out the "game" later.

## Art Direction

Cozy. Pastel-saturated. Simple and vibrant.
